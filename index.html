about myself

For as long as I can remember I have wanted to express my creativity by painting, drawing and designing. While at school, no text-book was safe. I couldn’t resist filling the back pages with sketches and mini works of art. After Schhol I studied all forms of Art & Design from Fashion, Photography, Painting and Pottery to Graphic Design.

At the age of twenty seven I made a bold decision to move from my native England to Sweden where I continued my studies in Computer Graphic Design - learning 3d modelling/rendering, Web Design, CAD and film-editing.

I have over 10 years experience working with Web Sites/Content, Social Media, Marketing Material and anything Graphical.
